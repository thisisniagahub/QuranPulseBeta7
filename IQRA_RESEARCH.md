## 2. IQRA 1-6 COMPLETE CURRICULUM BREAKDOWN

### Standard IQRA Method (by Ustaz Haji As'ad Humam)
- **Origin**: Yogyakarta, Indonesia (Pustaka AMM)
- **Method**: "Talaqqie Musyafahah" — direct reading without spelling first
- **Approval**: Checked & revised by **Malaysian Ministry of Education (KPM)**
- **Adopted by**: Malaysia, Indonesia, Brunei, all ASEAN countries
- **Target**: Children ages 4-7, can read Quran in ~8 months
- **Core Philosophy**: Phonic method — how Jibril taught Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) to read Quran
